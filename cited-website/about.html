Our Story

Built by young people, for young people.

CITED began as a small circle of friends who wanted to give teenagers what they wish they had: someone to listen, someone to believe, and a community to belong to.

Who we are

A growing movement for teen empowerment.

CITED — Community Initiative For Teens Empowerment And Development — is a newly established NGO focused on creating safe, inspiring spaces where young people can grow into confident, compassionate leaders.

We're small, we're honest about where we are, and we're building something that will outlast us — together with the communities we serve.

Mission

Empower teenagers to discover their voice, purpose and power.

Through mentorship, education and community projects, we help teens unlock the leaders already inside them.

Vision

A generation of young people leading meaningful change in their communities.

We dream of communities where every teenager has a mentor, a path, and a place to belong.

Our journey

2025 · GROW

Community outreach launches

School partnerships and weekend programs reach hundreds of teens.

2026 · BUILD

Leadership academy

Our first dedicated leadership track opens to teens across three regions.
